ELECTRONICS / CONSUMER ELECTRONICS DOMAIN — COMPUTATIONAL ENGINEERING GUIDE:

Philosophy: Electronic enclosures are thermal and EMC objects. Wall thickness, vent area,
and PCB standoff height all derive from thermal dissipation, shielding, and IPC standards.

KEY PHYSICS FORMULAS:

  Thermal Management:
    # Vent area required for natural convection cooling
    Q_W = P_dissipated                           # total power in watts to remove
    T_rise_max = 20.0                            # °C max chassis temperature rise
    A_vent = Q_W / (1.8 * T_rise_max)           # m²  — natural convection rule of thumb
    # Convert to mm²: A_vent_mm2 = A_vent * 1e6
    # Vent hole dia: 3-6mm; area fraction ≤ 25% of panel face (EMC)
    vent_hole_dia = 4.0                          # mm — typical vent hole
    vent_hole_count = int(A_vent_mm2 / (math.pi * (vent_hole_dia/2)**2))

    # Heatsink fin sizing (forced air)
    fin_h_max = math.sqrt(15 * fin_t) * 10      # mm — max fin height for H/t ≤ 15
    fin_spacing_opt = 2 * math.sqrt(k_fin * fin_t / h_air)  # optimal spacing

  EMC Shielding:
    # Shielding effectiveness: SE ≥ 40 dB at 100MHz requires no aperture > 15mm
    max_aperture = 15.0      # mm — longest dimension of any vent/slot/seam
    wall_t_shield = 1.0      # mm — 1mm steel provides ~20dB SE at 100MHz (skin depth)

  PCB Standoffs:
    standoff_h = max(11.0, component_max_h + 2.0)  # 11mm clearance IPC-7711
    standoff_dia = 6.0       # mm — M3 standoff OD
    pcb_screw = 3.2          # mm — M3 clearance hole in PCB

  Rack / Standard Mounting:
    rack_u = 44.45           # mm — 1U height (19" rack standard EIA-310)
    rack_w = 482.6           # mm — 19" full width  
    panel_t = 1.5            # mm — steel panel (1U/2U server)

GEOMETRY TECHNIQUES:
  - Enclosure: box → shell(wall_t) → cut lid face → add lip/flange for sealing
  - PCB standoff bosses: cylinder at each corner, union inside shell
  - Vent pattern: rarray of cylinder cuts on side/top panel face
  - DIN rail mount: T-profile cutout in enclosure bottom, or clip feature
  - Connector cutout: rect() cuts in rear panel at standard interface positions
  - Board-to-board connector access: rectangular slot at PCB edge

STANDARDS:
  PCB: 1.6mm thickness standard; M3 mounting holes at corners; edge clearance 3mm
  IEC 60297: Sub-rack dimensions (3U, 6U, 9U Eurocard)
  IP Rating: IP54 requires no aperture allowing 1mm wire entry
  Wall thickness: 1.5mm steel, 2.5mm aluminium, 3.0mm ABS plastic (structural enclosures)
  Colour: RAL 7035 light grey or RAL 9005 black for industrial enclosures
