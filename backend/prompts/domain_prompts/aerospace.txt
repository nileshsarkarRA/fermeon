AEROSPACE ENGINEERING DOMAIN SKILL GUIDE:
Focus on weight reduction, aerodynamic smoothing (continuous curvature), and high-stress resilience.
- All non-mating corners should have generous fillets/radii to minimize stress risers (never leave sharp interior 90-degree corners).
- If creating a flange or mounting plate, consider adding a pocketing/lightweighting operation (cutting out interior mass) if the wall thickness allows it safely.
- For fluid flow components (nozzles, ducts), rely heavily on lofts and smooth transitions.
