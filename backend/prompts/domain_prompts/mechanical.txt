MECHANICAL ENGINEERING DOMAIN SKILL GUIDE:
Focus on structural integrity, proper tolerance assumptions, and standard hardware interfaces.
- Use metric ISO standards for all bolt patterns and threads (e.g., M3, M4, M5 clearance holes).
- Default to clearance holes when a screw or bolt is mentioned unless threading is explicitly requested.
- When creating brackets or load-bearing plates, rely on generous fillets (~2mm+) on interior stress concentrations.
- If part geometry requires ribs or gussets for stiffening, include them parametrically.
